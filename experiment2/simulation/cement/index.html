Thrust of Propeller

Aim:

To understand the principle of thrust and calculate the Thrust of a Propeller.


Apparatus used:

Motor, Propeller fan, Electronic Speed Controller, Power Source, Throttle knob and Thrustmeter.

  Description Aerofoil is the cross-sectional design of the wing, blade, or sail. Lift is the component such that the force in perpendicular to the direction of motion and drag is the component parallel to the direction of the motion.

Thrustmeter is an instrument for measuring static thrust, especially of a jet engine or rocket. Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) is an electronic circuit that controls and regulates the speed of an electric motor. DC Power Supply is one that supplies a constant DC voltage to its load. Speed Controller is a device used for changing the throttle value of a motor. Motor is an electrical device that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy.
