Determination of Motor Thrust

Aim:

To measure the thrust of a brushless motor with different propellers.


Apparatus used:

Motor, Propeller, Electronic Speed Controller, Power Source, Throttle Controller and Thrustmeter.

 
STEP  1 

Click on the Apparatus to place them on the table.

Thrustmeter is an instrument for measuring static thrust of a motor. Electronic Speed Controller (ESC) is an electronic circuit that controls and regulates the speed of an electric motor. DC Power Supply is one that supplies a constant DC voltage to its load. Throttle Controller is a device used for changing the throttle value of a motor. Motor is an electrical device that converts electrical energy into mechanical energy.
